About Miracle Hearts

A safe, joyful space built around every child's unique journey.

Miracle Hearts Special School & Child Development Center is a specialised institution supporting children with Autism, ADHD, Down Syndrome, Learning Disabilities, and Developmental Delays. We believe every child is capable of extraordinary growth when given the right environment, patience, and personalised attention.

Our therapy teachers are highly experienced, compassionate professionals trained in modern evidence-based techniques. From the moment a family walks through our doors, we become partners in their child's journey.

Personalised Care

Every child receives an Individual Education Plan tailored to their unique strengths.

Experienced Faculty

Certified therapists with years of specialised training and heart.

Safe Environment

A warm, sensory-friendly space where children feel at home.

Our Services

Specialised support for every child's needs.

We provide comprehensive assessment and intervention for a wide range of developmental and learning conditions.

Autism Spectrum

Structured, sensory-aware programs that help children with autism build communication and life skills.

ADHD

Focus-building activities and behavioural strategies for children with attention challenges.

Down Syndrome

Warm, patient, milestone-based learning that celebrates every child's pace of growth.

Learning Disability

Multi-sensory teaching methods for dyslexia, dysgraphia and other learning differences.

Developmental Delay

Early intervention programs designed to bridge developmental gaps meaningfully.

Behaviour Issues

Positive behaviour support plans that help children manage emotions and grow confidently.
